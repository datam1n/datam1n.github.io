About DataMin

DataMin is a small research (or reading) group focusing on various aspects of intelligent data processing. It is a member of the Applied Computational Intelligence Research Center, Faculty of Mathematics and Computer Science, Babe»ô-Bolyai University.

We focus on issues related to

  • text mining
  • stochastic processes
  • robotics
  • deep learning
  • semi-supervised learning
etc.

Activity

We organise research seminars and reading group. In the research seminars we present and discuss recently appeared articles relevant to the research domain.

Software

  • Sparse Online Gaussian Processes Toolbox (Matlab)
  • teztyt - One-class multiple choice test generator and evaluator (Python)
  • Fake News Detection Without External Knowledge (Python code of the experiments)

Datasets

  • Last.fm + LyricWikia + MusicBrainz lyrics dataset
  • CiteSeerX 4179 dataset for record linkage and metadata extraction
  • Toy datasets for semi-supervised learning (4 datasets)
